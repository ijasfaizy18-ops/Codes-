Frequently Asked Questions
Can I order paint directly from the colour catalogue?
Yes, you can! Select any wall paint colour you want, provide details, and have it delivered to your doorstep. Before this, you can even buy a sample swatch if you’re unsure of the colours you want to buy.
How are your painting services different from others?
We offer a painting service with a “yes” mentality, accommodating your needs, family, and house rules to tailor the painting process to your routines and expectations.
How do I identify what my painting requirement is?
To identify your painting requirements, assess the scope and surfaces to be painted, consider surface condition and type, select appropriate paint type and finish, choose colours to match your style, and factor in budget and time constraints.
